About

I made this app because I saw my dad watching a 3 hour youtube video of a school board meeting from start to finish, and I thought that that process could be optimized. This tool can generate a searchable english transcript when none are available (even from foreign languages), and allows for querying that transcript with ChatGPT or with regex searching.

In more technical language, this app takes a youtube url, gets the best transcript (a manually entered english transcript, or a locally generated english transcript using OpenAI's Whisper model), chunks and vectorizes the transcript, and loads the most similar 2000 word chunk (using cosine similarity) into ChatGPT as context for a user prompt.

It basically takes a youtube video and answers any question you ask it, without bankrupting you with API fees.
